About Us

"Oyster Kode" is a coding club at Rajarambapu Institute of Technology, founded on January 13, 2020, under the coordination of Prof. A. B. Patil. Currently, Prof. A. D. Homkar serves as the club coordinator. Initially, the club started with just 6 founders but has since grown to include over 85 active members. In its early days, the focus was on fundamental computer science engineering concepts and programming languages. However, it has expanded its scope to cover various technologies and activities, including DSA, Web Development, Java, OOPs, C++, C, Canva, and more. The club is entirely student-driven, and it has successfully completed 2 batches, demonstrating its commitment to enhancing both technical and non-technical skills among students.
